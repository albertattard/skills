Q: Who is the first real user for the MVP?
A: An absolute beginner learning German from scratch.

Q: What first-level behaviour proves the app is useful?
A: The learner sees an image for a noun, hears the noun pronounced, sees the written noun, chooses from `der`, `die`, and `das`, and receives article-plus-noun audio after selecting correctly.

Q: What counts as completing the first level?
A: The learner correctly answers 25 distinct nouns.

Q: Should the PRD include the fixed initial 25-noun inventory, or only define the noun requirements and leave noun selection as a follow-up content task?
A: Define the noun model only.
